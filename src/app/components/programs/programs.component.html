Programs

We offer a wide array of programs from morning and afternoon classes to Montessori teacher training. Read more about our curriculum here.

Classes: Ages 2.5-6 yrs

Our mission is to provide students with a meaningful extension of their school day in a safe and familiar environment. Our programs consist of morning and afternoon classes, with options to extend the length of hours.

A Typical Weekly Schedule
  • Morning Class: 8:30 – 11:30 a.m., children participate in regular Montessori curriculum.
  • Afternoon Class: 12:30 – 3:30 p.m, children participate in regular Montessori curriculum.

Extended Classwork Options
  • Early Morning: 8:30 – 11:30 a.m., children participate in regular Montessori curriculum.
  • Late Afternoon: 3:30pm – 5:30pm, children play games, make music and art to close out their day.

Specialized Tutoring (for 5 years and up)
  • Bilingual Classes: 3:30 - 4:30 pm, Group or individual lessons for each subject: English, Korean.
  • Tutoring: 3:30pm – 4:30pm, Individual tutoring for math, language, homework and special education.

Lynnwood Montessori Teaching Training Program

We regularly hold Montessori teacher training programs. If you're interested, please email us!

The Lynnwood Montessori Teacher Training Program goals are to:
  • Prepare teachers to conduct early childhood education for ages 2.5 through 6 years with a deep grounding in Montessori's philosophy.
  • Develop teachers with Montessori training in the contemporary context of child development and educational thought.
  • Create teachers as “scientific pedagogues” understand the dynamic system that allows the child to pursuit of their intellectual and moral needs.

The Lynnwood Montessori Teacher Training program is an extension of Dr. Jun's Institute of Montessori Education in South Korea, which has been accredited by Montessori Accreditation Council of Teacher Education (MACTE) and Affiliated by American Montessori Society (AMS) since 2000.
