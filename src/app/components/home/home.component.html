Our Academy

The Lynnwood Montessori Academy provides offers a complete range of educational services for children (ages 2.5-6 years old), including care before and after school, and special summer programs. We pride ourselves in providing an enriching, multi-faceted environment where children thrive.

Children love to learn by experiencing the world using all of their senses. We provide a hands-on classroom environment stocked with learning materials for important area from practical life skills and sensory management, to mathematics and STEM, to art, music and language. Even the youngest children love learning about their world! Visit our programs page to learn more.

Our Philosophy

Our Montessori philosophy is rooted in the following.
  • Freedom of movement: To encourage a broad understanding of the world, we encourage the children to move about freely, choosing their “work” with responsibility and freedom. We nurture their independence and watch their self confidence grow as they learn through thoughtfully planned projects that allow them to learn as individuals, at their own pace and to reach toward their full potential.
  • Guidance for Learning: Our school is based upon the fundamental need of each child to have freedom of within limits. We structure our care so that children are given ample opportunity to explore their world with their hands in a safe environment so they may learn.
  • Socialization: As children learn, we encourage them to use their growing vocabulary and express themselves as a individuals. We love chatting with the children in our care, talking about their interests, life values and loved ones.
